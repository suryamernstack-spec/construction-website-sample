How A Project Moves

Four Phases,
One Team

Every build runs through the same four gated phases, so you always know exactly where your project stands.

01

Plan

Site survey, budgeting, and design development with your architect or ours — scope and cost locked before we break ground.

02

Permit

We manage the full entitlement and permitting process with local jurisdictions, so approvals don't stall your start date.

03

Build

Dedicated superintendent on site daily, weekly owner updates, and a live schedule you can check any time.

04

Deliver

Punch list, final inspections, and closeout documentation handed over — plus a one-year warranty walkthrough.
